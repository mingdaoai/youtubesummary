## Recommended Category Structure

For organizing these transcripts, I recommend using **3 primary categories** with optional tags for mixed content:

### Primary Categories:
1. **Investment (价值投资)** - Pure investment and stock analysis
2. **Entrepreneurship (创业经营)** - Business and entrepreneurship
3. **Life Philosophy (人生智慧)** - Life lessons and personal development

### Tagging System:
- Videos can have **multiple tags** if they span categories
- Mixed category videos can be filed under the **primary category** (the one with highest score) and tagged with secondary categories
